Customer Experience
Have you ever been on a website where time flew by without notice? I believe this happens because of human connection and good design. Design
I believe that good design can change the world. Achieving this change is not all about skill, but also about how a designer thinks. A designer must combine empathy for the context of a problem, creativity in the generation of solutions, and rationality to tailor solutions to the context. My training and life experiences have grounded me in these qualities, preparing me well to work with Fresh Tilled Soil.
